Year Poster Design 2

In this intriguing project, I delved into the historical events of the year 1980, uncovering the notable release of the first Pac-Man game in an arcade cabinet. Motivated by this discovery, I set out to design three variations that would pay homage to this iconic moment. The distinctive aspect of each variation lies in the overall style, where I deliberately chose to experiment with different fonts and diverse arrangements of images. This creative decision not only adds visual interest to the designs but also reflects a nuanced exploration of how typography and image composition contribute to the overall aesthetic. Each variation serves as a unique visual narrative, encapsulating the significance of the Pac-Man game in 1980 through creative and stylistic choices.
