Nature Poster Design

In this nature poster project showcasing Mount Everest, I explored diverse design techniques across four distinct designs. The chosen approach for one design involved overlaying images, amplifying the visual experience and creating a dynamic composition. Another design embraced simplicity, featuring a single image of the mountain with its name, aiming for an elegant and straightforward visual impact. For the third design, I maintained a clean and simple aesthetic while integrating additional information about the location. This design not only serves as a visual representation but also becomes an informative piece, offering viewers a glimpse into the details and significance of Mount Everest. The project as a whole reflects a versatile exploration of design techniques, catering to various visual preferences and delivering a well-rounded representation of the iconic natural landmark.
