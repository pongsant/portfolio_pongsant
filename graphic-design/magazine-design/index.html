Magazine Design

In this project, I designed a fashion magazine, infusing each page with creativity and giving it a distinct name. With a focus on the brand Balenciaga, driven by my admiration for its style, every page and cover features captivating visuals and insightful details about Balenciaga. This creates a comprehensive exploration of the brand’s influence in the fashion realm.

The magazine takes a unique twist on the last page, offering a brief history of fashion across different years as a captivating conclusion. This final touch provides readers with a broader perspective on the evolution of fashion. The magazine, as a whole, seamlessly combines contemporary style, brand appreciation, and historical insights, catering to the diverse interests of fashion enthusiasts.
