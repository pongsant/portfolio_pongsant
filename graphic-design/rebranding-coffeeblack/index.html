Rebranding Coffeeblack

Logo Design

In this project, I took on the task of crafting a fresh and impactful logo for a shop named “BlackCoffee.” The creative process involved generating a total of four distinct designs, each with its unique elements and visual appeal. The aim was to capture the essence of the coffee shop while ensuring versatility and memorability in the logo designs. Considerations such as color schemes, typography, and graphic elements played a crucial role in achieving a cohesive and engaging brand representation. The exploration of multiple designs allowed for a thoughtful selection, ensuring that the finalized logo would effectively resonate with the brand identity and leave a lasting impression on customers.

Rebranding Coffeeblack

In this task, I undertook a thorough rebranding initiative for the coffee shop “BlackCoffee,” extending across various elements such as the logo and assorted designs. The objective was to revitalize the visual identity of the establishment, ensuring a cohesive and appealing brand image. The logo redesign aimed for a modern and distinctive look, setting the tone for the overall rebranding strategy. Various design elements, including signage, packaging, and promotional materials, were revamped to align with the updated brand aesthetic. The comprehensive rebranding effort sought to enhance the coffee shop’s market presence, create a memorable visual impression, and elevate the overall customer experience.
