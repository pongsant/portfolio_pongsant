Soft Hands Concept Album Packaging & Poster Design

This project is a conceptual album and poster design created for a design assignment exploring the relationship between art and typography. The artist name and album title are entirely fictional and were created by me for this project. All visuals, typography, and design elements were also developed by me from concept to final layout.

I designed a full visual system including an album cover, tracklist layout, and promotional poster. The project focuses on mood and atmosphere, using soft light, gradients, and minimal typography to create a calm and emotional tone that reflects the imagined music style behind the album.

Skills: Visual Identity, Typography, Layout, Art Direction
Deliverables: Album Cover, Poster, Tracklist Layout
Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator
