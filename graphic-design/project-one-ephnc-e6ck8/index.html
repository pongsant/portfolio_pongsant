7 Sins Design

This project involved using Illustrator to design a poster. The instructor instructed me to select one of the seven deadly sins, and I chose greed. I depicted this sin by incorporating images of money and portraying a person being attacked by a devil-like figure, symbolizing the consequences of living with the sin of greed.
